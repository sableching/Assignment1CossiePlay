CossiePlay Party

On the first Saturday of each month, we hold a "CossiePlay party at the town hall from 6pm to 11pm. There will also be a live DJ taking song requests for all our customers to choose their favourite tunes to dance to! Feel free to wear your favourite costume and join us for a night of fun!

Entry fees:

  • Adults (18 & above): $15
  • Children (6-17): $10
  • Children (under 6): Free
Each entry comes with a non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick.

Those interested can register to come for a party by emailing us at cossieplay@gmail.com, with details including their name, phone number and the number of people coming.

Party Guidelines

  1. Party-goers party at their own risk and are responsible for their own safety.
  2. Party fees must be paid at the foor (by Cash or EFTPOS). Credit card payments attract a 2% surcharge.
  3. Alcohol is served to over 18 year olds only.
  4. Intoxicated people will not be served, and will instead by escorted to a taxi - which the party-goer must pay for.
