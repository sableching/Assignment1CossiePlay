About CossiePlay

CossiePlay is a family-run business that has been operating in Hulkstown since 2012. We can create any custom costumes our customers order - from a simple elf costume to Ironman costumes with functioning force fields. Depending on the difficulty of the costume, they can take between 1 to 50 weeks to be completed and cost anywhere between $10 to $10,000.


Second-hand costumes are available for sale as well under 'Costumes'.


CossiePlay also holds monthly parties. Please check out the 'Cosplay Party' tab for more information.
